This project develops a wireless-powered full-azimuth podded thruster for unmanned vessels. It targets sealing reliability and maintenance challenges in conventional podded propulsion systems by transferring power wirelessly into the sealed propulsion unit.

Key responsibilities included wireless power transfer system modeling and parameter design, high-frequency wireless power converter control and integration, motor drive control, sealed mechanical structure design, 3D modeling, prototype integration, debugging, and validation.

System highlights include a 1200 W / 100 V GaN motor driver, a 5 kW integrated WPT inverter, a 5-10 cm transfer distance, 92% peak efficiency, and a sealed mechanical structure.
